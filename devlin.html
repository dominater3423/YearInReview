Janet Devlin

Background

She was born in somewhere in rural Ireland. At, I believe 16, appeared on X Factor where she was a favorite of the judges and made it pretty far. After that agencies tried to sign her, but she decided to have a go at it as an independent artist.

Confessional

This is her new album, which from what I've heard looks quite good. Holy Water is apart of it, which may just be the best thing she's done. The theme of it is exactly what it sounds, a confessional. Which means the songs are more personal. I've always liked songs that combined folk and pop, which this does with its Celtic roots.
